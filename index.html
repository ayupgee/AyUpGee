👋 About

Hey, I'm Gee 🐝

I'm a Manchester-based content creator and streamer with an enormous soft spot for cosy games, wholesome communities, and a really good brew.

I started streaming because I wanted a space that felt genuinely relaxed: no pressure, no toxicity, and no pressure to be anything other than yourself. Just someone sitting down with a good game and a chat that actually talks back. That's still what AyUpGee is all about.

My world mostly lives in Disney Dreamlight Valley: the DreamSnaps, the valley designs, the character quests, all of it. I also love Animal Crossing, life sims, cosy indie games, and the occasional Fortnite session when the vibes demand it.

If you're looking for community-first, lurker-friendly, wholesome streams where chat actually matters. Pull up a chair. Grab a brew. You're home. 🫖
