👋 About

Hey, I'm Gee 🐝

I'm Gee, and I go by AyUpGee. I'm a Manchester-based content creator and streamer with an enormous soft spot for cosy games, wholesome communities, and a really good drink.

I started streaming because I wanted to build a genuinely relaxed, friendly, wholesome space — somewhere with no pressure, no drama, and no need to be anything other than yourself. Community always comes first here, and lurkers are just as welcome as anyone else.

My world mostly lives in Disney Dreamlight Valley: the DreamSnaps, the valley designs, the character quests, all of it. I also love Animal Crossing, life sims, and cosy indie games. The streams are warm, friendly, and a little chaotic in the best way.

If you're looking for community-first, lurker-friendly, wholesome streams, you've found your spot. Grab a drink and settle in.
